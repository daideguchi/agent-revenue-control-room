## Submission Story

People are starting to work with AI agents the way teams work with employees.
That is useful, but a solo builder still needs to know:

- what the agent did
- how much it might cost
- what evidence exists
- what needs human approval
- what another human or AI should do next

Agent Revenue Control Room is the missing control layer.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Pick the Alibaba Function Compute deployer item.
3. Show the evidence ledger and cost guard.
4. Generate a review packet through the live Alibaba Function Compute endpoint.
5. Choose approve, request proof, or stop.
6. Show the action log.
